The Brief
Design a conceptual poster for Singapore Design Week and produce the brochures for it.

The Concept
When it comes to designing, there are essential tools to designers - their adobe softwares, wacom tablet, french curve rulers, anything that would help them achieve their vision. Without these, it is probably impossible for them to create something. Therefore I wanted to portray these tools in a typography form for Singapore Design Week.
