About the Publication
The iconic monuments of Singapore. The neoclassical architectures that carries many stories that helped shaped Singapore into what it is today. The historically rich edifices that were Designed to Stay. This publication brings you through a journey back in time of 3 monuments that laid the foundation of Singapore.
The Concept
In this publication, I wanted to showcase three of the most iconic historical buildings of Singapore - the Old Supreme Court, Victoria's Concert and Theatre Hall and the Fullerton Building.

Black and white images were used to illustrate how far back these buildings go, and to put focus on the intricate details of the building by minimalizing the colors. Throughout the publication, a triangular, circle or rectangular motifs can be seen in each chapter, where it is paired with a building that has the same properties of that shape, as well as to represent the buildings as a "foundation."
