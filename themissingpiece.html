The Missing Piece

Project Type
Advertising, User Interface Design
Softwares
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Experience Design, Adobe After Effects
Year
2017
The Brief
This is a simulated project conceptualising the smart-bike sharing system under a subject called 'Studio Project.' My task was to promote this smart-bike sharing system that would be implemented in Singapore by the Land Authority of Singapore (LTA), and design the mobile application user interface that would go along with it.

The Concept
During the research process, I found that majority of the public were not aware of this existing bike sharing system that has been implemented in other countries. Therefore my advertisement would focus on bringing about awareness on this system. By conducting a survey, I found that the public would enjoy this service (after getting to know about it) due to its advantages, such as that it covers the first and last mile journey of many commuters, which is particularly what I focused on as a selling point in the advertisement.


Print Ads




Isometric illustration style is used in the aesthetic throughout the different deliverables. The print ads illustrates how the smart bike-sharing system is "The Missing Piece" that bridges the gap between 2 places, commonly known as the first and last mile journey.
