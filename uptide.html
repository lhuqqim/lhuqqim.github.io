Brief Information
For this particular work, my partner and I had to find a problem and create a mobile application to solve the problem. The problem we went with was that surfing apps that are already out on the market are not user-friendly, and most of the app's designs are outdated. So we created an app that is more user-friendly, and introduced a feature whereby users can hire an instructor for surfing lessons through the app. The app also serves as a purpose of fitness tracker by allowing users to make reports of their surf. Reports can be done automatically (without the need to key anything in) by pairing their mobile phones with a wearable device while surfing.
